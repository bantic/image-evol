Experiment to compare the speed of shrinking an image with WASM or in plain JS.

The shrink algorithm is: iterate through the pixels of the image and average contiguous blocks of pixels.

Timings are logged in the console. Sample results on 2/19/19 for a 500x500->100x100 shrink:
Shrink with JS takes ~15-25ms (but multiple calls appear to cause Chrome's optimizing compiler to kick in, and subsequent shrinks take ~3ms). Shrink with WASM takes ~65-75ms.
